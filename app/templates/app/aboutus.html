Our momos are made with love

"To make steam momo, start by preparing the filling. Combine ground meat (such as chicken, pork, or beef) with finely chopped vegetables and seasoning. Traditional seasonings may include ginger, garlic, onions, and a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Mix everything well to ensure even distribution of flavors. Next, prepare the dough for the momo wrappers. Mix all-purpose flour with water and a pinch of salt to form a smooth, pliable dough. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into thin circles. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each dough circle. Fold the edges of the dough over the filling and pleat them together to seal the momo. Repeat this process until all the filling and dough are used up. Once the momo are assembled, steam them in a steamer basket or steamer pot for about 10-15 minutes until the dough is cooked through and the filling is hot and juicy. Serve the steam momo hot with a side of dipping sauce, such as a spicy tomato chutney or a tangy soy-based dipping sauce. Enjoy!"







Taste the difference with our handcrafted momos

"To make fried momo, start by preparing the filling. Combine minced meat, such as chicken, pork, or beef, with finely chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and a mix of spices like cumin, coriander, and chili powder. Mix well to ensure the flavors are evenly distributed. Next, prepare the dough by mixing all-purpose flour with water and a pinch of salt until it forms a smooth, elastic dough. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into thin circles. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each circle, then fold and seal the edges to form half-moon-shaped momo. Heat oil in a deep frying pan or pot and carefully fry the momo until they turn golden brown and crispy on all sides. Once fried, remove the momo from the oil and place them on paper towels to drain excess oil. Serve the fried momo hot with a side of dipping sauce, such as spicy tomato chutney or soy-based sauce. Enjoy the crunchy texture and delicious flavors of fried momo!"





Our momos are the perfect blend of tradition and innovation

"To make open momo, begin by preparing the filling. Combine minced meat, such as chicken, pork, or lamb, with finely chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, and chili powder. Mix the ingredients thoroughly to ensure even seasoning. Next, prepare the dough by mixing all-purpose flour with water and a pinch of salt until a smooth, elastic dough forms. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into thin circles. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each dough circle. Fold one side of the circle over the filling, leaving the other side open to create a half-moon shape. Repeat the process until all the filling and dough are used. Steam the open momo in a steamer basket or pot for about 10-15 minutes until the dough is cooked through and the filling is tender. Serve the open momo hot with a side of dipping sauce, such as tomato chutney or sesame sauce. Enjoy the delicious flavors of open momo!"
