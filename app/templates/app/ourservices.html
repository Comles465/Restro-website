Our momos are made with love

"To make steam momo, start by preparing the filling. Combine ground meat (such as chicken, pork, or beef) with finely chopped vegetables and seasoning. Traditional seasonings may include ginger, garlic, onions, and a blend of spices like cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Mix everything well to ensure even distribution of flavors. Next, prepare the dough for the momo wrappers. Mix all-purpose flour with water and a pinch of salt to form a smooth, pliable dough. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into thin circles. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each dough circle. Fold the edges of the dough over the filling and pleat them together to seal the momo. Repeat this process until all the filling and dough are used up. Once the momo are assembled, steam them in a steamer basket or steamer pot for about 10-15 minutes until the dough is cooked through and the filling is hot and juicy. Serve the steam momo hot with a side of dipping sauce, such as a spicy tomato chutney or a tangy soy-based dipping sauce. Enjoy!"
